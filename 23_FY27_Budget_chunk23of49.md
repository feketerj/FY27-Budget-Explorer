This PE line also supports manufacturing development tasks to support the Army's SkyFoundry initiative and goal to scale drone production within the Organic Industrial Base (OIB) by 10X.  Tasks in this Program Element support manufacturing development, research, development, and test efforts for munition payloads to deliver lethal & protective effects from sUAS platforms.  Lethal & Protective Payloads for sUAS: The Army has immediate need for lethal effects delivered by uncrewed systems to maintain overmatch against competitors that have unprecedented access to lethal, low-cost autonomous systems. The Lethal & Protective Payloads project will enable sUAS delivered effects through the Picatinny Common Lethality Integration Kit (CLIK) which consists of physical, electrical, and data interface design standard that creates a modular ecosystem of compliant hardware modules to enable rapid integration of an sUAS delivered munition suite. The Picatinny CLIK ecosystem consists of a Weapon Release System (WRS), Fire Control Module, Fuze Module, Radio Module, Power Module, and adapters for various common platforms and payload types. The munition suite will consist of legacy and commercial munitions to sUAS delivered lethality (e.g, dropped, launched, fired, or emplaced). The resulting lethal & protective capabilities delivered from sUAS will be anti-personnel, anti-materiel, obscuration, hazard neutralization, and deception.
