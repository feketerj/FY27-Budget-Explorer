The project will develop Terrain Shaping prototype systems leveraging an accelerated agile acquisition strategy that includes rapid prototyping followed by system of system demonstration, collaboration and fielding to Transformation in Contact (TiC) units to mature Terrain Shaping technologies in real time to drive innovation, enhance learning, refine requirements, and accelerate solution development. The Terrain Shaping systems will use an open system and modular approach to leverage existing Army infrastructure and capabilities to include Command, Control, Communication, Computers, Cyber, Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (C5ISR) platforms, Robotic Ground Vehicles, and Unmanned Aerial Systems.
